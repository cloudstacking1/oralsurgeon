What is oral surgeon?

An oral surgeon, also known as an oral and maxillofacial surgeon, is a dental specialist trained to diagnose and treat complex issues involving the mouth, teeth, jaws, and face. While a general dentist handles routine check-ups and fillings, an oral surgeon is the expert you turn to for advanced procedures like tooth extraction, wisdom teeth removal, and surgical tooth extractions.
